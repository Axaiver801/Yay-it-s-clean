Our Mission

At Yay it’s Clean, our mission is to bring families and pet owners peace of mind through safe, pet-friendly cleaning that removes odors, stains, and everyday messes. More than a cleaning service, we deliver an experience — saving you time, money, and hassle with convenient pickup and drop-off options. As a veteran-owned business, we’re committed to serving our Utah community with the same dedication, discipline, and care that defined our service — helping every home feel fresh, welcoming, and truly clean.
